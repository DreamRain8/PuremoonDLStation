## Social Card/Role Cultivation

Such games are music games with character cards and cultivation gameplay as the main core. Although they also have classic music game gameplay, their importance is not as high as that of traditional single-type music games.

Character cards are an important gameplay mechanism of the game. In the music game gameplay, the impact of character cards on the score is far higher than the judgment of the player's technical level (number of perfect judgments, number of Miss, number of combos, etc.).

Compared with music games, the experience of such games is moreÂÅèÂêë‰∏∫ RPG (role-playing games), but the combat/running elements are converted into music game gameplay.

However, such games still have situations where the player's music game strength is required (various difficult songs), and there are also rank assessment elements (such as Bang Dream!'s ex cards, PRSK's Kaimen, etc.), so ordinary music game players can also enjoy fun in them.
